Top 5 Video Game Franchises You Should Try Out

Looking For a New Game to Play?

Try playing a game from one of these franchises! We'll even include some game recommendations for each franchise. Happy Gaming!


  1. 1. The Legend of Zelda

    2. Do you love fantasy worlds? Do you like exploration and epic combat? If you said yes, then The Legend of Zelda is the franchise for you!


    3 Games to Try Out:

    • The Legend of Zelda: Breath of the Wild, Nintendo Switch (2017)
    • The Legend of Zelda: A Link to the Past, SNES (1992)
    • The Legend of Zelda: Ocarina of Time, N64 (1998)

  2. 2. Persona

    3. Ever wanted to experience the daily life of a Japanese high-schooler? Do you like RPG's and turn based combat? Persona blends your typical RPG game with elements of Social Simulation! Experience Japanese school and make friends during the day, and at night, fight monsters with your party. Level up your social stats and relationships with your friends to get more powerful abilities.


    3 Games to Try Out:

    • Persona 3 Reload, Xbox, PS4,PS5, Nintendo Switch 2 (2024)
    • Persona 4 Golden, Xbox, PS4, Nintendo Switch (2012)
    • Persona 5 Royal, Xbox, PS4, PS5, Nintendo Switch (2019)

  3. 3. Fire Emblem

    4. Fire Emblem is a tactical battle game. Guide your party in epic battles! However, be careful! If one of your units die in battle, they won't return for the rest of the game!


    3 Games to Try Out:

    • Fire Emblem: The Blazing Blade, Game Boy Advance (2003)
    • Fire Emblem: Three Houses, Nintendo Switch (2018)
    • Fire Emblem: Path of Radiance, Gamecube (2005)

  4. 4. Metroid

    5. Step into the suit of galactic bounty hunter Samus Aran, and explore alien planets as you fulfill your mission.


    3 Games to Try Out:

    • Metroid Prime Remastered, Nintendo Switch (2023)
    • Super Metroid, SNES (1994)
    • Metroid Dread, Nintendo Switch (2021)

  5. 5. Sonic the Hedgehog

    6. Race through levels as the blue blurr himself, Sonic. Sonic games are unique platformers that focus on maximizing speed!


    3 Games to Try Out:

    • Sonic Advance 3, Game Boy Advance (2003)
    • Sonic Adventure 2, Dreamcast, PS2, Gamecube (2001)
    • Sonic X Shadow Generations, Xbox, PS5, Nintendo Switch, Nintendo Switch 2 (2024)
